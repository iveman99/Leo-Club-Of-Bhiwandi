About Leo Club of Bhiwandi

Leo Club of Bhiwandi is a youth leadership organization under Lions Clubs International dedicated to community service, leadership development, and fellowship. The club focuses on empowering young leaders to create positive change in society through service initiatives and community engagement.

President’s Note

It is a privilege and a moment of great pride to serve as the President of the Leo Club of Bhiwandi. Leading this incredible group of passionate young leaders has been a journey filled with learning, service, and a shared commitment to making a meaningful difference in the lives of others.

This year, our guiding belief has been simple yet powerful: to be the hands that help others rise and stand stronger. Service, for us, is not just about extending help; it is about empowering individuals, restoring hope, and reminding our community that they are never alone. Every initiative we undertook was driven by compassion, unity, and the desire to uplift those around us.

The Leo Club of Bhiwandi is more than an organization; it is a family bound by the values of Leadership, Experience, and Opportunity. Each project we execute, every effort we make, and every smile we bring reflects the collective dedication of our members who believe in serving with sincerity and heart.

The visit of our esteemed District President is a proud and memorable occasion for our club. It is an opportunity for us to reflect on our journey, present our efforts with pride, and receive valuable guidance that will inspire us to continue striving for excellence. Such moments remind us that we are part of a greater movement of young leaders committed to building a better and more compassionate world.

I extend my heartfelt gratitude to every Leo member, our guiding Lions, advisors, and supporters whose encouragement and dedication have been the backbone of our success. Your unwavering belief in service continues to inspire our club to grow stronger with every step we take.

As we move forward, let us continue to lead with compassion, serve with humility, and stand together in our mission to uplift those in need. When our hands unite for service, even the smallest efforts can create the greatest impact.

“The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others.”

Yours in Leoism,

Leo Yeman Adep

President
